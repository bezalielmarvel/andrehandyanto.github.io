Andre Handyanto is an Indonesian fashion designer based in Paris working in the fields of fashion, textiles, and accessories.

Studied at IFA Paris, Andre discovered his interest in working for ready-to-wear creation, he started his first job at Emmanuelle Khahn Paris.

He treasures the values of hardworking, details, and perseverance while having the minimalist, elegant, and clean style over his work experiences. He has strong interest in visual storytelling, poetic words, and keen individuals.

He works for some known brands at Paris such as Emanuel Ungaro, Mary Katrantzou, Moon Young Hee, Vivienne Westwood, Pascal Millet, Emmanuelle Khanh, and Pandamaki.
